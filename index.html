Writings inspired by oneness

Open Source Life is a little collection of writings on the joy of embodiment and technology as a means to express that. With tech playing an integral part in our sense of community, we'd like to bring more playfulness to it—inspiring a generation to view coding as a beautiful means of self-expression.
